Rules

Game Types:

There are four game types viz, 'Country to Flag', 'Country to Capital', 'Flag to Country' and 'Flag to Capital'.

In 'Country to Flag' you will be given a country name and you have to chose the correct flag of that country from the given choices.

In 'Country to Capital' you will be given a country name and you have to chose the correct capital of that country from the given choices.

In 'Flag to Country' you will be shown a flag and you have to chose the correct country name from the given choices.

In 'Flag to Capital' you will be shown a flag and you have to chose the correct country capital from the given choices.

Game Modes:

There are four game modes viz, 'Arcade', 'Quiz', 'Time Trial' and 'Never Say Die'.

In 'Arcade' mode you will be given a series of questions. Your aim is to select the correct answer for all the questions. You will be given a total of three lives and each time you chose an incorrect option you will be awarded a penalty of one life. The game ends when you exhaust all your lives.

In 'Quiz' mode you will be given a set of ten questions and your aim is to select as many correct answers are possible. Unlike the previous mode, the game finishes only when you have attempted all the questions.

In 'Time Trial' mode you will be qiven a series of questions which has to be answered in a limited amount of time. The quiz ends when the time gets over.

In 'Never Say Die' mode you will be qiven a series of questions which you can answer at you ease. The round goes indefinitely until you give up.
